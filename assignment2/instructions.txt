This app is a simple music game where the user needs to click on the notes on the screen to get points. 
The game will play a piece of music and the notes will appear with the rhythm of the music and the user needs to click on the notes to get the score. 
At the end of the game the user's final score is displayed.

-Use the Pygame library to implement the game's graphics and audio processing.
-Uses the Librosa library to analyse the rhythm of the music and generate notes.
